## Cliff vs the 300 MHz aggressive target (PLAN §2)

- Grid points reaching fmax ≥ 300 MHz: **0 of 6** (none).
- Peak fmax across the whole grid: **61.85 MHz**.
- The classic-mode PE array is combinational-depth-bound (one 10-lane dot product + cascade add per stage), so fmax plateaus ~50 MHz almost independent of size — there is no fmax-vs-size *cliff* in this range; the array never approaches the 300 MHz target.

## Fast Forward / Hyper-Retiming limiting reason (all points)

- `clk: Insufficient Registers` — 12/24 points
- `clk: Meets timing requirements: No further analysis performed.` — 12/24 points

Register-starved: the reused L0 MAC cell is single-cycle combinational, so the Hyper-Retimer has nothing to move — which is exactly why the LV1 (reset-style) delta is ~0 except at the smallest, cascade-free 1x2 point. Full interpretation in `rtl/microbench/l1_pe_array/README.md`.
